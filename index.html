Hi. I’m Darren Angle. I am an artist and writer.

I learned to write poetry and be a thoughtful human at Macalester College and Brown University, where I was a Seaver Fellow and creative writing instructor.

I was the Director of Digital Operations at Upshot Agency, where I produced large amounts of code and digital marketing strategy for several companies, including Google, Starbucks, Proctor and Gamble, KraftHeinz, Liftmaster, Nuveen Investments, and Constellation Brands (Corona, Modelo).

Currently, I run a company called You Should Quit Your Job.
As you may have guessed, we help people quit their jobs.
(Yes, it is the best job ever.)
