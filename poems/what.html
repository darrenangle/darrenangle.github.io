what do you know

I was hired by a person in a dark red robe
to guard a bag of black and silver gears
until some time in the future
when I have to return the bag

these gears will try very hard to escape
but you mustn't let them, no matter what
my hiring agent said
resting his thumbs on the rope at his waist

if you do, you will no longer qualify
for employee benefits

for several days
this was an easy job
and then a portal made of lighting and fog
opened up in the corner of my office

a person who looked very much like me
stepped through the portal and said
listen to me very carefully

I could tell that this person was the future me
by the increased muscle tone and confidence
and I looked forward to making people listen
to what I needed to say

I need a single gear from that bag, Future Me said
and I can't tell you why but you have to trust me
and by me I mean yourself you should trust yourself

do you love me I asked my future self

what?

do you
love me as in do you love who you used to be

look, if you don't believe in me we are both doomed

but I could lose my employee benefits
what if I get cancer

you won't
